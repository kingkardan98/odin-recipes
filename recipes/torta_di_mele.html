Italian apple pie


Ingredients (serves 4)

  • Apples: 930g
  • Sugar: 200g
  • All purpose flour: 250g
  • Butter: 100g
  • Whole milk: 150g
  • Eggs: 2
  • Lemons: 1
  • Yeast: 16g
  • Pulverized cinnamon: half a teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 pinch
  • Powdered sugar: to taste

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave, and set aside.
  2. Grate the lemon's skin, and juice the lemon. Store separately.
  3. Cut the apples in half, then remove the skin and the core.
  4. Quarter the apples, then slice along the short side.
  5. Set the apple slices in a bowl, then pur the lemon juice over, so the slices don't go brown.
  6. Sift the flour and the yeast together.
  7. In a big bowl, place the eggs and some of the sugar. Beat thouroghly with a hand mixer, while pouring all the sugar little by little.
  8. When the mixture gets clearer, add some salt, then keep going until the mixture is fluffy.
  9. Pour the butter, the cinnamon and the lemon skin in the mixture.
  10. When all the spices are incorporated, start slowly pouring in the flour.
  11. When the flour is incorporated, start pouring in the milk.
  12. When the mixture is fully incoporated, strain the apples and add them to the mixture.
  13. Coat the apples well, then place everything in a cake pan.
  14. Preheat the oven to 180 Â°C, then cook the pie for 55 minutes.
  15. When cooked, coat with the powdered sugar, then wait 30 minutes for it to cool down a bit.
  16. Serve and enjoy!
