Pasta carbonara


Ingredients (serves 4)

  • Spaghetti: 320g
  • Pork cheek: 150g
  • Egg yolks: 6
  • Pecorino romano cheese: 50g
  • Black pepper: to taste

Steps

  1. Add salted water to a pot, and set it on the stove until it boils.
  2. Cut the pork cheek into small strips.
  3. Put the sliced pork cheek into a hot pan for 10 minutes, until crispy and cooked.
  4. Start cooking the spaghetti in the pot.
  5. Put the egg yolks in a bowl, add the pecorino cheese and the black pepper, then mix until it reaches a creamy and aerated consistency.
  6. Remove the pork cheek from the pan, making sure to leave the juices in the pan.
  7. Once the pasta is almost (but not fully) cooked, drain it and put it in the pan.
  8. Add the egg mixture and the pork cheek back in, and cook fully.
  9. Plate, grate some more pecorino on top and enjoy while hot.
