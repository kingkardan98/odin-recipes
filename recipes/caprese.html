Caprese salad


Ingredients (serves 4)

  • Buffalo mozzarella: 150g
  • "Cuor di bue" tomatoes: 2
  • Cherry tomatoes: 100g
  • Fresh basil: 10-12 leaves
  • Salt, oregano, extravirgin olive oil: to taste

Steps

  1. Slice all the mozzarella and set in a bowl.
  2. Slice all the "cuor di bue" tomatoes and set in a bowl.
  3. In the tomato bowl, add salt, oil and oregano, and mix.
  4. In the mozzarella bowl, add just oil and oregano.
  5. In a separate bowl, put salt, oil and oregano over the cherry tomatoes, whole.
  6. Create layers in every plate: one slice of mozzarella, one of tomato. Do this until satisfied.
  7. Set some cherry tomatoes on the plate, then decorate with some basil leaves.
